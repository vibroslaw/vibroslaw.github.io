Rap-Ort

Long-form witness, conscience and historical pressure.

Rap-Ort is the institutional and long-form axis of Piotr Lichwała / Vibrosław’s work: an audiovisual project built for attentive screening contexts, cultural programming and educational reflection.

Truth does not need noise. It needs to be heard.

Orientation

This is not a story of heroes. It is a story of choices.

Rap-Ort sits between narrative music, cinematic language and the ethical form of witness. Its purpose is not to simplify history, but to restore weight, listening and responsibility.

What It Is

A long-form audiovisual structure

Rap-Ort is positioned for institutions, cultural partners, educators and reflective audiences who need more than a short promotional description.

Witness as form

Rap-Ort is not a concert, not a conventional fiction feature and not a sequence of music videos. It is a disciplined audiovisual structure in which report, narrative music, image and silence meet around witness, conscience and historical responsibility.
