SZTAB category

Forgotten

Names, stories, and achievements restored to memory.

Forgotten gathers those people, achievements, and historical threads that were pushed to the margins of memory or remembered too weakly compared to their real significance. It is a space of restoration — not through dry exposition, but through music, image, and concentrated narrative form.
