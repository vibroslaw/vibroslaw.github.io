Cinematic Historical Series

SZTAB

Episodes about resistance, memory, and the moments that shaped history.

SZTAB is an evolving series of short music-film works. Each episode stands as a self-contained story — about a figure, a neglected name, or a battle that changed the course of history. It is a series built for broader audiences without surrendering gravity, atmosphere, or respect.

Series Orientation

History does not survive by itself. Someone has to carry it forward.

SZTAB organizes its stories around three axes — Original, Forgotten, and Battles — in order to build not a playlist, but a durable world of memory and narrative.
