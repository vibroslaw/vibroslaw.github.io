Projects shaped by memory, witness, and responsibility.

Piotr Lichwała (Vibrosław) creates audiovisual works built from testimony, narrative music, and cinematic form — developed for attentive audiences, institutional presentation, and reflective viewing.

This work is not only about the past. It is about what responsibility asks of us now.
