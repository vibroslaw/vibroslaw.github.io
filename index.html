Projects at the intersection of memory, history, and responsibility.

Piotr Lichwała (Vibrosław) creates audiovisual works built from testimony, narrative music, and cinematic form — designed for attentive audiences, institutional contexts, and reflective viewing.

This work is not only about the past. It is about what responsibility asks of us now.
